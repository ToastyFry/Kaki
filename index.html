About Kaki!

Kaki! is a local discovery platform for affordable hidden gems in Singapore — small businesses run out of homes, indie cafes, hawker stalls, craft workshops, and micro-SMEs.

The word "Kaki" means "buddy" or "companion" in Singlish — someone who's always up for an adventure. We're your kaki for discovering the best-kept secrets in your neighbourhood.
